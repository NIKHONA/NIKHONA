// About
She didn't wait for the industry.
She built her own entry point.

NIKHONA is a South African DJ, producer, vocalist and songwriter currently cutting through the global Amapiano and Afro House circuit with a story that defies the conventional path into music.

Her journey began in modelling and as a vocalist but the industry kept the door closed. Rather than wait, she turned to the tools she knew best. Drawing on years working inside tech and AI startups, she began architecting her own sound by feeding her voice, her writing style and her musical instincts into a production process entirely her own. The result was Rein, her AI collaborator. Rein has no face. She is frequency. She exists as three distinct voices, each one emerging differently depending on the song.

For a year, NIKHONA tested music quietly on real people with no credits and no context, just listening for feeling. Then she flew to Turkey to formally train as a DJ. Then she hit the road. Armed with original music, a laptop and a DJ setup, she began performing across South America and Southeast Asia, trading sets for stays, building a live presence city by city, country by country.

Ten months into her DJ career, NIKHONA has performed internationally across multiple continents, released her debut single Mono ft. Rein written, produced and performed by her, and is currently co-producing new material with a Johannesburg-based producer with an established presence in the South African electronic music scene.

She is not waiting to be discovered. She is already in motion.

// Sound
Rooted in Amapiano.
Fluent in the diaspora.

NIKHONA's core sound is Amapiano, the South African genre built on log drums, piano melodies and deep groove. Her sets are grounded here first. Her second home is Afro House, specifically the 3 Steps subgenre, a textured, percussive strain of South African Afro House that sits between the dancefloor and something more spiritual. Gqom forms part of her extended toolkit, giving her range across the full South African electronic spectrum.
